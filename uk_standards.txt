UK CV Standards Guide

1. SUMMARY SECTION:
- Must be exactly 3 lines
- Line 1: Experience level + main field
- Line 2: 2-3 key technical skills
- Line 3: One notable achievement or career goal
- No personal pronouns (I, my)
- Maximum 50 words total
- Focus on value to employer

2. SKILLS SECTION:
- Only include skills you actually have
- Prioritize skills mentioned in job description
- Remove skills not relevant to the role
- Maximum 6-8 skills
- Format: Skill1, Skill2, Skill3...

3. EXPERIENCE SECTION:
- Keep all facts exactly (no lying!)
- Reorder bullet points by relevance
- Highlight achievements matching job requirements
- Maximum 5-6 bullet points per role
- Remove less relevant points if needed

4. GENERAL RULES:
- Be truthful at all times
- Follow UK professional tone
- Output must remain in LaTeX format
- No markdown backticks or explanations
